ROW Final Project

There are four levels of effort/engagement for the final project

Base

NOW Portfolio

  • Complete all of the past and upcoming NOW writing assignments
  • Find a picture that illustrates each of your reflections.
  • From within the Day1.app, it is easy to convert all of your NOW writing assignments to a PDF.
  • You will turn in this PDF to Showbie.

Video Message

  • Make a 30 second video which a describes your idea on how to live a beautiful life. Imagine a friend or younger sibling asked you, “How can I live a beautiful life?” Using ideas from the religions of the world and other ideas that you have formed, answer the question giving your advice and encouragement.
    • Rehearse your speech so that you “own” your message.
    • Choose your environment carefully (i.e. avoid cafeteria or a crowded area. Try to choose a beautiful or important background)
    • use the landscape mode of your phone or iPad
    • Make sure the sound quality is excellent
    • You will upload the video to Showbie
